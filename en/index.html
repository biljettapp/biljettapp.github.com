Get started with Biljettapp

  1. Forward your SMS-ticket to +4676-686 21 79
  2. Biljettapp replies with a link to your ticket
  3. Open the link & Add to Passbook

Standard text message rates apply.

Biljettapp is not SJ

Johan and Erik is developing Biljettapp without official support from train operators (like SJ or Norrtåg) and Trafikverket. We support tickets from Blå Tåget, Länstrafiken, SJ, Tågab, Tågkompaniet and Öresundståg.

Please write to us on Twitter or send a email to hej@biljettapp.se.

Passbook is just the beginning

We are still developing the service and during TravelHack we will build smart train ticket apps for Android & iOS.
